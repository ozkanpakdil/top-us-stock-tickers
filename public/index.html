Indicator guide

RSI
Measures the speed and size of recent price moves to flag overbought (>70) or oversold (<30) conditions.
HRSI
A faster, less lagging version of RSI built on the Hull Moving Average; readings below 35 suggest weak momentum and above 65 strong momentum.
MFI
Combines price and volume to gauge buying or selling pressure; high values mean heavy buying, low values mean heavy selling.
Stoch %K
Compares the latest close to the recent high-low range to show where momentum is heading and when a reversal may be near.

Status: Oversold / Overbought = extreme levels for RSI, MFI and Stoch; Weak / Strong = momentum strength for HRSI.
